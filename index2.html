Spiritual Essence

The Legend of Aarti

The Ganga Aarti is not just a ritual; it is a bridge between the human and the divine. Performed daily at dusk by young priests draped in saffron, it involves the chanting of Vedic mantras and the rhythmic movement of large fire lamps (Deepams) to honor Mother Ganga, Lord Shiva, and the Sun God.

Featured Ritual

Subah-e-Banaras

Experience the mystical sunrise at Assi Ghat. "Subah-e-Banaras" is a morning cultural and spiritual awakening that begins with Vedic Yagya, followed by classical music and yoga sessions as the sun rises over the Ganges.

Important Info

Pilgrim Guidelines

Dress Code

While there is no strict uniform, modest clothing covering shoulders and knees is recommended out of respect for the sanctity of the ritual.

Photography

Photography is allowed. However, please do not use flash directly in the faces of the priests. Drone photography requires special police permission.

Reporting Time

Traffic near Godowlia is heavy in the evenings. Please reach the reporting point by 5:00 PM to ensure you don't miss the boat departure.
